Who We Are

About Panatolya

Panatolya — a short form of Pan-Anatolia — was established in 2016 in Istanbul, Turkey as the International Division of Barakat Trading Co., bringing together over 20 years of experience in the trade and distribution of active pharmaceutical ingredients, chemicals, packaging materials and equipment.

We source raw materials and substances to the Middle East countries such as Turkey, Syria, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Lebanon and others — from worldwide reliable suppliers with whom we have established successful long-term partnerships.

We ensure strategic and high-performance sourcing through careful selection of materials and substances that fully conform to the applicable regulations. Our reliable quality assurance system forms the base for our successful partnership with customers and suppliers alike.
